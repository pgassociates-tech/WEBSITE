Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Prakhar Gupta & Associates, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of client information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard personal and professional data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 and ICAI guidelines.

Information We Collect

  • Basic contact details (name, email, phone number)
  • Business and financial information shared for advisory or compliance purposes
  • Documents required for statutory filings and regulatory compliance

Use of Information

The information provided is used strictly for professional services including tax advisory, audit, assurance, compliance, and regulatory representation. We do not share client data with third parties except as required by law or regulatory authorities.

Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to safeguard client data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. All records are maintained with professional confidentiality standards.

Client Rights

In accordance with the DPDP Act, clients have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of their personal data. Requests can be made by contacting us directly at prakhargassociates@gmail.com.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in law, regulation, or professional practice. The latest version will always be available on our website.
